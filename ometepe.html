Ometepe island

Due to the eruption of two volcanoes from the Lake of Nicaragua, created a big island named Ometepe Island, this island is situated in southwest Nicaragua. Is The biggest island in the lake.two massive volcanoes makes its topography. In 2010, a Biosphere Reserve designation was given to the island. Something that you can do in this island are: 1. Rent a motorbike or scooter to explore the island 2. Hike Conception Volcano 3. Swim at Ojo de Agua 4. Go on an adventure to San Ramon Waterfall 5. Relax at El Pital Chocolate Paradise
